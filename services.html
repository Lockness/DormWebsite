Services

The QuadSquad provides four packages of cleaning services.

The first option is the Monthly-Single Clean Dorm option: a regularly scheduled cleaning appointment provided each month on the day of your choice for a total of $16.99 for one roommate.

The second option is the Monthly-Double Clean Dorm option: a regularly scheduled cleaning appointment provided each month on the day of your choice for a total of $30 ($15 per roommate).

The third option is the Monthly-Triple Clean Dorm option: a regularly scheduled cleaning appointment provided each month on the day of your choice for a total of $45 ($15 per roommate).

The fourth option is the Emergency-Clean Dorm option: a same-day scheduled cleaning appointment provided on the day of scheduling for a total of $20 per roommate.

All of the above Clean Dorm options provide these services:

  • Vacuum
  • Kitchenware cleaning
  • Desk Cleaning and Polish
  • Garbage removal and bag replacement
  • Clothes Organization
  • Temperature adjustment
  • Window cleaning
  • Wire Organization
  • Smell Control
