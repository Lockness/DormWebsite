Single Appointments

The QuadSquad provides four packages of cleaning services.

Single Clean-Dorm option: One room clean provided for a single OR one roommate in a double/triple for a total of $16.99 for one roommate.

Double Clean-Dorm option: One room clean provided for a double OR two roommates in a triple for a total of $30 ($15 per roommate).

Triple Clean-Dorm option: One room clean provided for an entire triple (3 total roommates) for a total of $45 ($15 per roommate).

Emergency Clean-Dorm option: a same-day scheduled cleaning appointment provided on the day of scheduling for a total of $20 per roommate.


Fall Packages

Fall Semester Monthly Package: 4 scheduled cleans provided every 4 weeks from the date selected for the remainder of the semester.

Singles are only $60, doubles are only $100, and triples are only $170

Fall Semester Bi-Weekly Package: 8 scheduled cleans provided every other week for 16 weeks starting on the date selected.

Singles are only $120, doubles are only $200, and triples are only $340



All of the appointments and packages include the following services:

  • Vacuuming
  • Kitchenware cleaning
  • Desk Cleaning and Polishing
  • Garbage removal and bag replacement
  • Clothes Organization
  • Temperature adjustment
  • Window cleaning
  • Wire Organization
  • Smell Control
