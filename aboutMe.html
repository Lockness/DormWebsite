My story

Hi everyone, I’m Justin, 3rd year finance major at OSU and founder of The QuadSquad. Before I make your lives simple and rooms spotless, I thought I’d introduce myself. I’m a Jets, Yankees, and Knicks fan, but above all, the proudest Buckeye in the land. I was born and raised in Westfield, NJ where I learned the importance of hard work and dedication. As an honest, responsible guy, I’m on a mission to make my fellow Buckeyes’ lives easier and rooms cleaner.

My freshman year at Smith Steeb was where I noticed a demand for a dorm room cleaning service at OSU. Regular class schedules paired with social gatherings can make any room a disastrous mess. There aren’t enough hours in the day for a typical Buckeye to keep a tidy room. Between homework, exams, clubs, and nights out, a dirty room is an unnecessary evil.
So, please let me help you. For $30 a month ($15 per roommate), The QuadSquad will keep your room clean so you can keep your grades up.
