Frequently Asked Questions


We understand if you have a couple


  • 1. Who cleans the room?
  • A fellow student from The Ohio State University who has gone through a thorough background check and has been quality tested.

  • 2. Do I have to be present?
    • The university recommends escorting the cleaning team into the dormitory for security purposes. However, after entry to the room, you are free to come and go as you please as your dorm is being cleaned.

  • 3. Do I have to provide any materials for cleaning?
  • No, all necessary cleaning supplies will be provided by the cleaning team.

  • 4. What’s included in each clean?
  • For 30 minutes, the cleaning team is yours. Each team follows a detailed 12-point checklist of required work, however, we encourage you to inform the team of any special requests before any cleaning period.

  • 5. Can I make up a clean I miss? (vacation, tardiness, exam, etc.)
  • Any scheduled clean can be made up if you contact us before 24 hours of your cleaning appointment. Within 24 hours, however, requires a late cancellation fee which will be added to your next clean’s bill.
